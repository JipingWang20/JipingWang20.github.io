Research

Matrix-Response Regression with Low Tubal Rank and Sparsity

Jiping Wang, Xin Zhang

Under review · 2026

A unified framework integrating low tubal-rank and sparsity constraints for structured high-dimensional data. Proximal gradient and linearized ADMM algorithms with convergence guarantees; applied to EEG seizure data identifying P4, C4, T4 as the strongest biomarkers.

t-SVD · tubal rank · ADMM · EEG

Hierarchical Sure Independence Screening for Multiresolution Classification

Jiping Wang, Qing Mai, Xin Zhang

In preparation · 2026

HierSIS — a scalable screening method for high-dimensional multinomial classification preserving hierarchical response structure. Superior reliability over penalized competitors across p ∈ {1500–5000}; applied to PBMC scRNA-seq data with K=24 cell types.

HierSIS · multinomial · scRNA-seq

Censored Mean Variance Sure Independence Screening for Survival Data

Wei Zhong, Jiping Wang, Xiaolin Chen

Computational Statistics & Data Analysis · 2021

Master's research at Xiamen University. A model-free feature screening method for ultra-high-dimensional survival data under censoring, with sure screening and ranking consistency. Near 100% true-variable inclusion under 20–40% censoring.
